MisterBlue's Books I've Read

I still read those paper based things. Here are the books that I have read in the last year or so. Clicking on the cover of book will take you to the Amazon page for that book where you can get other reader's comments and, if the mood hits you, purchase the book.

To ease your cognitive load, the books are rated with zero to four "Roberts". A rough guide:

Four Roberts : Buy it. Find it, Read it. Good, good, good.
Three Roberts : Well, every author can't be Shakespeare or Huxley. There are interesting characters or maybe an interesting place. In general, a good book.
Two Roberts: You are standing in the book store about to fall for the same title or cover art that I did. You will read the book and conclude it's not the best literature you have read but, on the other hand, you have also read much worse. You are trapped in an airplane or bored at the beach and this book will fill the time.
One Robert: you will have regrets in the morning.
Zero Roberts: why did the publisher bother? Don't be an accessory to the crime.
